TWO GAMES IN ONE

The fun is adversarial price discovery, not gambling.

GAME 1

Price the Lot

Read the signals. Estimate the value. How close can you get? This is information analysis — the more you understand prediction markets, the sharper your edge.

GAME 2

Price the Players

What will they bid? Are they aggressive? Cautious? Bluffing? The real long-term skill is reading the room, not just reading the lot.
